Original Milk Tea

CAD $ 6.5

The tea: We used Tetley’s orange pekoe black tea for its strong flavour and rich colour.
The milk: In this case, we’re not using real “milk”. We chose to use Coffee Mate’s non-dairy powdered creamer for a richer and milkier flavour and a slightly richer texture.
The sweetener: We used regular granulated white sugar because we want to keep the sweetener flavour neutral.

R:5.0 L:5.5
