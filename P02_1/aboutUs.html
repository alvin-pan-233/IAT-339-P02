About US

BubbleOrzo is founded in 1997 by our Chairman Tommy Hung. Through 22 years of development, BubbleOrzo has became the global leading brand in the bubble tea industry.
As our franchisees seeks to provide drinks that give people sparks of happiness and warmth, BubbleOrzo support our partners and franchisees worldwide with our professional training programs, well-designed evaluation system, innovative products, and the assistance of our highly experienced management team.
BubbleOrzo continuously dedicate ourselves in innovating and progressing to build a sustainable and profitable business system. Up till today, we never cease to develop new product lines, upgrade the customer experience, and broaden the accessibility to our brand, thus penetrating into the worldwide markets, and becoming the global leading brand.

From Canada to the World

Through high standard of quality, consistency, and service, we have established more than 4000 stores around the world. We provide creative tea drink including: fresh tea, milk tea, fruit tea, yogurt drink, coffee and other beverage products, to drink lovers around the world.
